# Context

## Glossary

**Platform Admin** — the installation operator who creates Organizations and appoints their Owners. _Avoid_: Organization Owner, admin.

**Organization** — an album-selling enterprise or independently managed business unit with its own members and products. _Avoid_: customer group, discount group.

**Organization Owner** — an Organization member who administers membership and may appoint additional Organization Owners. _Avoid_: Platform Admin, admin.

**Manager** — an invited Organization member who runs day-to-day operations without membership administration authority.

**Staff** — a future operational Organization member with narrower permissions than a Manager.

**Customer** — an invited Organization member who can view only that Organization's customer-facing products.

**Membership** — a User's role-bearing relationship with one Organization; one User may have memberships in multiple Organizations.

**Product** — an Organization's configurable sellable offering, such as an album or frame.

**Configuration Draft** — a Customer's resumable, incomplete selection of Product options before checkout. _Avoid_: cart, Order.

**Order** — a Customer's submitted purchase of one configured Product in a chosen quantity. _Avoid_: Configuration Draft, cart.

**Project Name** — an optional Customer-provided label that identifies a Configuration Draft and its resulting Order independently of the system-generated Order Number.
